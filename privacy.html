Privacy Policy

Last updated 25 June 2026.

Duckdoku is a cozy logic puzzle game. This policy explains what we store and why. We do not show ads, and we do not sell your data.

What we store

  • Game progress. Your level, stars, boosters, and settings. This is saved on your device. When you play inside Telegram, your progress is also saved to your Telegram account so it follows you across devices.
  • Telegram profile basics. When you play inside Telegram, the game receives your Telegram user id, first name, username, and language. We use these to save your progress and to send optional game reminders.
  • Purchases. If you buy boosters, the payment is processed by Telegram Stars. We keep a record of the item and amount so we can deliver what you bought.
  • Anonymous analytics. We use Mixpanel to collect anonymous usage events, for example levels started and completed, plus your device type and an approximate region from your connection. This helps us improve the game and is not used to identify you personally.

How we use it

To run the game, save your progress, deliver purchases, send optional reminders, and improve the experience.

Who we share it with

We use Telegram for the platform and payments, and Mixpanel for anonymous analytics. We do not sell your information to anyone.

Notifications

The bot may send optional reminders to come back and play. You can turn them off at any time by sending the command stop to the bot.

Children

The game is family friendly and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Deleting your data

You can delete your data at any time. See duckdoku.com/delete-account.

Contact
